Course Unit: Mental health care II

The following are the topics and subtopics to be covered in Mental health care II

Organic Mental Disorders

1. Diagnose and manage HIV/AIDs related disorders

2. Diagnose and manage Alcohol, Substance use and related disorders

3. Diagnose and manage Epilepsy

4. Diagnose and manage Mental Retardation

5. Diagnose and manage Delirium

6. Diagnose and manage Dementia

Guidance and counseling in psychiatry

1. Identify mental clients who require Guidance and counseling

2. Counsel mental client, attendants and family members

3. Give Health education talks

Psycho-Social Support services

1. Occupational and rehabilitation therapy

2. Medical social skills

3. Medical Sociology

4. Carry out community visits

5. Conduct community sensitization

Child and Adolescent Psychiatric Disorder

1. Behavioral Disorders

2. Conduct Disorders

3. Emotional Disorders

4. Learning Disorders

5. Feeding and elimination Disorders
