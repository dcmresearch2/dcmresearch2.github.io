How to Download the App

1. Connect your phone to internet.

2. Use your browser to Visit "dcmdownloadpage.github.io" and click on the download button.

3. If you receive a google protect message saying that the app might be harmfull to your phone, just ignore it and proceed to downlod the app.

4. Now wait untill the app is fully downloaded to your phone...it might take just a few minutes

How to Install the App

1. Now that the download is finished, click on the App APK file to install the app.

2. if a message appeares telling you to allow installation of apps from external sources, please do so by visiting your settings-scroll below and check the option for allowing installation of apps from external sources.

3. now the app will install successfully into your phone and you are good to go..just start reading your notes and prepare for exams
